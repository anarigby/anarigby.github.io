Welcome to my webpage for EDID6508 – Developing Instructional Media! Here, I’ll be diving into the knowledge and skills needed to create impactful instructional media. This course emphasizes the development of socio-technical abilities, essential for designing media tailored to online and e-learning platforms. Come along as I explore the creation of media artifacts that aim to enhance digital learning experiences..

My Philosophy on Instructional Media

My philosophy for instructional media is that it should use technology and creative design to make learning clear and engaging. Good media combines tools like videos and visual aids to help students understand and apply new concepts effectively. The goal is to create impactful and accessible learning experiences.
